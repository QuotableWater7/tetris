Music Hack Day - Denver


Overview

My goal coming into the hackathon was to gather some ideas for generating music automatically for internet games. My apps use the Echo Nest "Dynamic Playlisting" API to play songs in a specific genre and tempo based on what is happening in the game.
The Apps
  • Tetris v1 - increases the tempo of the played song each time the user clears a row. In reality, I would have the tempo increase with levels... but I used line clearing so I could see the effect much quicker.
  • Tetris v2 - in this version, music is selected based on how close the user is to losing. When blocks are near the bottom, I select rock tracks with a tempo of around 90bpm. In the middle, I select techno tracks at about 140bpm. Near the top, I start throwing some metal tracks at you with a tempo of about 200bpm.
